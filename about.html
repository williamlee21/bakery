My Story

Hello! Welcome to the website for Dollie's Bakery that is located in NYC. My name is Jennifer Au-Yeung or my friends know me as Dollie. My story starts off as somebody who was on a quest to find herself. I worked as a registered dental hygienist for six years and loved it. But I always felt like something was missing from my life. It wasn’t until after I had my son that I took on baking as a fun hobby. It was an area where I could express my creativity that I was suppressing for so long. I became a stay at home mother who wanted to become an inspiration for her son.

When I started baking macarons, there was a satisfaction that I received from it that I could not explain. They looked so decadent and elegant in every way. I couldn’t believe that I was able to create such a beauty in my kitchen. From there, I started serving them to friends and family members and it became a sensational hit. Everybody loved them and raved about how I should sell them. Even those who don’t like sweets at all in my family wanted more. That was when I knew I wanted to pursue further with my macaron adventure.

Every macaron that I make is made with love and special care. I’m a one-woman baker who makes sure that every macaron is perfectly filled and a work of art before it leaves my hands.
